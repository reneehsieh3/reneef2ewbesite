Hi, I'm Renee — an explorer at heart and a designer driven by curiosity. I grew up in a warm family in Taiwan, where I learned the value of empathy, open-mindedness, and persistence. My parents, both lawyers, taught me to think critically and express myself clearly, while my younger sister — five years my junior — constantly reminds me to stay playful and kind.

As an ENTP, I thrive on discovering new ideas and connecting unexpected dots. I've always been fascinated by how things work — whether it's through building, sketching, or experimenting with new materials. Over the years, I've found joy in playing the violin, practicing calligraphy, and painting — creative outlets that let me express emotions words can't capture.

I love traveling and exploring unfamiliar places, not only to see the world but to understand different perspectives and find inspiration in the small details of everyday life. Of course, my path hasn't always been smooth — there were moments of doubt, challenges in balancing creativity with practicality, and times I questioned my direction. But each experience has helped me grow stronger, more grounded, and more curious than ever.
